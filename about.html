Company History

WITHX Innovations began as a small, focused effort to turn engineering ideas into working solutions and has since grown into a dedicated space for research, design, and product development.

From an idea to a working studio

WITHX Innovations was founded with a simple belief: engineering ideas shouldn't stay on paper, they should become real, usable products. What started as early experimentation and prototyping soon grew into a structured engineering studio built around research, industrial design, and hands-on execution.

Today, WITHX operates from a dedicated workspace where the team collaborates on prototypes, product design, and R&D projects — bringing together engineering discipline and a startup mindset under one roof.

Every milestone along the way, from the first patent to the first working prototype, has shaped WITHX into a company built on innovation, integrity, and real-world impact.
