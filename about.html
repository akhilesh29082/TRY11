Company History

WITHX began as an idea shaped by curiosity, creativity, and the desire to solve real-world problems through engineering. It grew from a founder-led journey of exploring innovation not only as an academic pursuit, but as a practical force that can improve systems, products, and everyday experiences.


What started with a student innovator’s determination gradually evolved into a startup vision. Through experiments, concepts, patent-focused thinking, and a commitment to execution, WITHX developed into a company built around product innovation, engineering value, and long-term impact.


The journey reflects a strong entrepreneurial mindset: starting small, thinking big, and consistently translating engineering ideas into tangible opportunities.
